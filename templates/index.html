ISP

Made for ISP course.

Our project is a multi-use software tool, which can be applied in a quantity of situations. We supply our clients with the service of visual based human profiling. Given by an image or a stream of images (video) information about the acting persons is given. The extracted data is the target person's gender, age, mood, and possible value for a certain business
